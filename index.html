What you get

Everything your app needs
to manage its users.

NexAuth is the backend you drop into your app. Your users register, log in, and get access to whatever tiers you define. You manage everything from an embedded admin panel — no separate dashboard, no extra logins.

👥
User Accounts for Your App
Your end-users get accounts with usernames, passwords, and emails. They register directly inside your app — NexAuth handles everything behind the scenes.
🔑
Tiered Access Control
Define free, premium, and admin tiers for your users. Gate any feature in your app with a single line: auth.is_premium(). Upgrade users instantly from your admin panel.
💻
HWID Locking
Bind your users' licenses to their hardware automatically. Prevent account sharing. Reset any user's HWID from your admin panel when they get a new PC.
⏱️
Trial Management
Grant time-limited trials to your users — minutes, hours, days, or weeks. Trials expire automatically and revert to free. Extend or revoke anytime from your admin panel.
🛡️
Ban & Suspend Your Users
Temporarily suspend a user with a duration and reason, or permanently ban them. Your users see your message when they try to log in. Lift it with one click.
📍
IP Login Tracking
See the last 3 login IPs for every one of your users. Spot account sharing, suspicious logins, or compromised accounts. Admin accounts are always protected and redacted.
🔐
Password Reset Flow
Your users request a reset and you get notified. Issue them a code through your admin panel — no email server needed. Codes expire in 48 hours automatically.
🎛️
Embedded Admin Panel
Your entire admin panel lives inside your app, visible only to admin-tier accounts. Manage all your users without leaving your app or maintaining a separate dashboard.
📶
Offline Grace Period
Your users stay authenticated for up to 7 days without internet. Their tier and session are cached locally so your app keeps working when they're offline.
Simple by design

From zero to protected app
in 5 minutes.

NexAuth plugs into any Python desktop app. Two files and a config change — that's the entire integration.

1
Pick a Plan & Deploy
Choose your plan, click deploy to Render. Your dedicated auth server and Postgres database are live in under 2 minutes.

Drop Files Into Your App
Add auth.py and login_window.py to your project. Set your server URL. That's literally the entire client integration.
3
Gate Your Features
Use auth.is_premium() anywhere in your code to unlock features for paying users. One line per feature.

Ship & Manage
Distribute your app however you want. Your embedded admin panel lets you manage every user — tiers, bans, trials, resets — without leaving the app.
